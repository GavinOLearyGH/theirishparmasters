Round Setup

Enter Your Round Details for Analysis

Choose your course mode, set the round details, and give TIP the context it needs to read the card properly. Then enter the scorecard, analyze the round, and decide whether to clear it, save it, or turn it into the next practice block.

How to Enter

Legend

  • Fairway → Y (hit) or L / R / S / O (miss: left / right / short / long)
  • Green → Y134 (hit from 134) or L125 / S140 (miss + distance)
  • Putts → 1–4
  • U/D, S/S → Y or N
  • Keep it quick — clean input reveals better patterns
Quick Prompts

What this tool is looking for

  • Is the card getting hurt by penalties, three-putts, or a wide tee pattern?
  • Is there a one-way miss, a two-way miss, or simply a weak distance window?
  • Which part of the round held up well enough to keep?
  • What is the one practice block that would most improve the next card?
